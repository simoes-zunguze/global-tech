ABOUT

Globo Tech is a privately held Mozambican company that specializes in Engineering, Procurement and Construction attending areas of Control & Instrumentation, Eletrical services, Electronic Safety & Security, Solar Systems whilst adhering strictly to the client's safety, quality, and cost requirements. Globo Tech service and sell a wide range of capital and industrial equipment for Mozambique's most important industries including Oil & Gas, Mining, Processing, Material Handling, Power Generation and Oil terminals. Representing the best Brands in Control, Instrumentation, engineering and renewable energies; supported by a group of highly qualified Professional Engineers, Globo Tech is capable of engineering, installing, and maintaining a wide range of industrial equipment and systems.

MISSION

Offer customized products and services working together with the client to achieve high levels of sustainability, and reducing losses through the application of the best industrial standards, always seeking results that equal the cost benefit.

VISION

We aim at high levels of excellence in the application of engineering and in the provision of industrial products and services through innovation, providing a unique experience that exceeds the expectations of our customers.
